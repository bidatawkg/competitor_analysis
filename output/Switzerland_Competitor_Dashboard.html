Market Trends

Customer Preferences

Switzerland has a strong culture of gambling, with a long history of traditional casino games such as poker and roulette. In recent years, there has been a significant shift towards online gambling due to convenience and accessibility. The availability of a wide range of games and betting options online has contributed to this growth.

Mobile Gambling

One of the key trends is the increasing popularity of mobile gambling. With widespread smartphone and tablet use, more customers are opting to gamble on the go. Mobile gambling platforms offer a seamless and user-friendly experience, allowing customers to place bets and play games anytime, anywhere.

Live Dealer Games

The emergence of live dealer games has gained significant traction in Switzerland. These games provide an interactive and immersive experience, allowing customers to play with real dealers in real-time. This trend combines the convenience of online gambling with the authenticity of traditional casino games, particularly appealing to customers who enjoy the social aspect of gambling.

✓ Macroeconomic Factors

  • Internet Penetration: High internet penetration rate drives online gambling growth
  • Strong Economy: High per capita income and stable financial system support leisure spending
  • Cultural Acceptance: Gambling is widely accepted as entertainment in Swiss society
  • Regulatory Clarity: Clear licensing framework creates controlled and secure market
  • Social Contribution: Gambling revenues support AVS and cantonal projects (culture, social, sports)
